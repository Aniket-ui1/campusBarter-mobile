# CampusBarter — Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
**Version:** 1.0 | **Effective Date:** March 2025 | **Applies To:** All registered SAIT students

---

## 1. Purpose
This Acceptable Use Policy governs how students may use the CampusBarter platform. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

---

## 2. Eligibility
- You must be a **currently enrolled SAIT student** with a valid `@sait.ca` or `@edu.sait.ca` email address.
- You must log in using your **SAIT Microsoft account** (Azure AD / Microsoft Entra ID).
- Sharing your account with others or creating multiple accounts is **not permitted**.

---

## 3. Permitted Uses ✅

Users MAY:
- Post genuine skill offerings or requests related to academic or professional development
- Contact other students to arrange skill exchanges
- Use Time Credits to compensate other students for their time
- Report inappropriate content or suspicious behaviour

---

## 4. Prohibited Uses ❌

Users MAY NOT:

| Prohibited Activity | Consequence |
|---|---|
| Post false, misleading, or fraudulent listings | Immediate account suspension |
| Harass, threaten, or abuse other users | Permanent ban |
| Attempt to exchange currency for Time Credits | Account termination |
| Post content that violates SAIT's Student Code of Conduct | Referral to SAIT administration |
| Attempt to access another user's account or data | Legal action + permanent ban |
| Upload malicious files or attempt SQL/script injection | Legal action + permanent ban |
| Scrape or automate requests to the API | IP block + account termination |
| Post personal contact information publicly | Content removal |
| Use the platform for commercial advertising | Content removal |

---

## 5. Time Credits Policy

- Time Credits are an **internal unit of exchange only** — they hold no monetary value.
- Credits cannot be transferred to cash or external payment systems.
- CampusBarter reserves the right to adjust or reset credit balances in cases of abuse.
- Time Credits expire **12 months** after last account activity.

---

## 6. Content Standards

All listings and messages must:
- Be **relevant to academic or professional skill exchange**
- Use **respectful, professional language**
- Not contain personal contact information in public listings (phone, email, social media)

---

## 7. Moderator and Admin Authority

- **Moderators** may remove any listing that violates this policy without notice.
- **Admins** may suspend or permanently ban accounts for policy violations.
- All moderation actions are logged in the AuditLog for transparency.

---

## 8. Enforcement

Violations are handled as follows:

| Severity | Action |
|---|---|
| Minor (first offence) | Warning via in-app notification |
| Moderate | 7-day suspension |
| Severe / Repeated | Permanent ban + SAIT Student Services referral |
| Illegal activity | Account termination + report to appropriate authorities |

---

## 9. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated at any time. Users will be notified of material changes via in-app notification. Continued use of CampusBarter constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
